Partner Organizations

The Monteverde Institute (MVI)

A non-profit educational organization founded in 1986 in the mountainous Costa Rican community of Monteverde. The MVI was established to guide the increased tourism in a positive and productive manner for the benefit of the visitors and local community. The MVI accomplishes this goal by integrating academic programs, research, and community initiatives into dynamic programs.

ProNativas

ProNativas is a network of scientists, biologists, landscapers, and gardeners from Costa Rica concerned about the low diversity of native plants and the excess of exotic plants in gardens. Some widely used exotic plants escape from gardens and invade wooded habitats and wilderness areas.
