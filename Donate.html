Donate & Support

All tax donations through the Alliance for the Monteverde Institute are tax-deductible in the United States of America. The following are all the availables methods by which we accept donations.

Checks

If you are donating from within Costa Rica, you can deposit a check to the Monteverde Institute at the following address:

Instituto Monteverde,
APDO 69-5655,
Monteverde, Puntarenas,
Costa Rica

If you wish to send a check from the US to the AMVI for the US tax deductible contribution, please mail your check to the following address:

Ashley Gora, Treasurer Alliance for the Monteverde Institute
8985 SW Washington Dr.
Portland, OR 97223
United States of America

Direct Deposit

If you would like to make an electronic funds transfer to our Banco Nacional (Costa Rica) account, please send an email directly to Seidy Torres (seidy@mvinstitute.org).

PayPal

If your preferred method of donation is through PayPal, please click the button below. Note that the Paypal system is set up for donations that go directly to the Monteverde Institute. If you need US tax deduction status for your donations, please send the donation directly to the Alliance for the Monteverde Institute.

JustGiving

If your preferred method of donation is through JustGiving, please click the button below. Please note that JustGiving will retain a percentage of your donation. More of your dollars go directly to the Monteverde Institute when you donate with a check sent by postal mail.
