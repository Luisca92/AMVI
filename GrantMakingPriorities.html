Grantmaking Guidelines

Eligibility

The Alliance for the Monteverde Institute (AMVI) does not accept unsolicited proposals. The AMVI considers proposals on an invitation-only basis. If you believe your organization/program is a good candidate for AMVI to consider for a grant, we ask that you send a letter of inquiry through our contact form. In this letter please describe your organization and proposed project as well as its relevance to the AMVI's mission statement. In addition, please provide an estimate of the funds that would be requested. Once invited to apply, applicants will be provided further instructions directly from AMVI.
